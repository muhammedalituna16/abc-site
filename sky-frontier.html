Sky Frontier

Build your airship empire among the clouds. Explore floating islands, survive deadly storms, and battle sky pirates in this high-altitude adventure.

About the Game

Sky Frontier is a survival-adventure where you craft, explore, and conquer the skies. Navigate your crew through hostile weather and dangerous airzones.

From aerial combat to sky-trading, experience a mix of tactical action and peaceful discovery, all aboard your customizable airship.

Key Game Modes

Story Campaign

Uncover the truth behind the Lost Skies and ancient cloud civilizations.

Free Roam

Explore islands and skies at your own pace with full airship control.

Co-op Multiplayer

Play with up to 4 friends. Assign roles, manage resources, and conquer together.

Sky Arena

Battle other airships in real-time PvP combat across floating battlefields.
