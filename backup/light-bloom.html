Guide the light through mirrors and make the plants bloom!

OBJECTS

Light Source

The sun emits light from a fixed position.

Seed

The plant that awaits the light to come to life.

Game Board

Played on a grid.

Obstacle

Obstacle: Light cannot pass and you cannot place a mirror on this tile.

Mirror (A) — 45°

Fixed angle at 45°. Cannot be rotated; can be moved to other grid squares.

Mirror (B) — -45°

Fixed angle at -45°. Cannot be rotated; can be moved to other grid squares.

Static Mirror (A)

Fixed and not movable. Angle is predetermined and locked.

Static Mirror (B)

Fixed and not movable. Angle is predetermined and locked.

Hammer

Tap an obstacle to smash it.

Flipper

Tap a mirror to flip its type.

CHARACTERS

VEGAS

THE HERO: A curious red panda with endless courage! Vegas guides the light through every challenge and brings life to the plants. Without him, the journey cannot begin.

LUMO

THE TECH GENIUS: A clever fox with glowing blue energy! With his smart visor and sharp mind, Lumo finds the perfect angles to solve even the hardest puzzles.

IRIS

THE VISIONARY: A wise owl with a calm spirit! Iris always sees the bigger picture, guiding the team through darkness with knowledge and foresight.

BLAZE

THE BRAVE: A fearless tiger with fiery determination! Blaze leaps into danger without hesitation and inspires the team with his strength and speed.

TERRA

THE PROTECTOR: A gentle but strong guardian of nature! Terra defends the roots of life, clearing the path and keeping the team safe through every obstacle.

PIXEL

THE ADVENTURER: A cheerful robot with a glowing screen face! Pixel brings energy and humor to the journey, turning every error into a new opportunity.
