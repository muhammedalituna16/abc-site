About the Game

Embark on a quest for ancient secrets in forgotten lands. Crystal Hunters invites you to a world filled with mysterious dungeons, dangerous monsters, and legendary treasures. Forge your own path and become the greatest hunter in these lands.

Choose from characters with unique abilities, collect and upgrade your gear. Embark on a solo epic adventure or form a guild with friends to challenge the toughest foes. Your destiny is yours to write.

Key Features

Vast World to Explore

Discover a huge map full of secrets and adventures.

Strategic Combat

Combine different skills to gain an edge over your enemies.

Legendary Gear

Collect and craft hundreds of different weapons, armors, and magical items.

Guild System

Join forces with your friends and defeat the most challenging bosses.
