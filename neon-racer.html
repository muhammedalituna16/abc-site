Neon Racer

COMBINE THE NEON LIGHTS OF THE 80S WITH THE SPEED!

About the Game

In this race where the legendary 80s aesthetic meets a futuristic cyberpunk atmosphere, push your reflexes to the limit. Prove you're the fastest driver in a metropolis illuminated by neon lights.

Modify your vehicle with the points you earn in each race, unlock new tracks, and write your name at the top of the global leaderboard. Get ready for an adrenaline-filled experience accompanied by retrowave music!

Key Features

Mind-Blowing Speed

A sense of speed that pushes the limits and fluid gameplay.

Retrowave Soundtracks

Immersive and licensed music fitting the 80s theme.

Leaderboards

Compete with players worldwide and aim for the top.

Extensive Customization

Customize your car's color, rims, and neons to your taste.
