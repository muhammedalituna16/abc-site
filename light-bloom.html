Reflect. Grow. Solve. A tranquil puzzle game where sunlight meets life.

About Light Bloom

Light Bloom is a cheerful and calming puzzle adventure. Use fixed mirrors to guide sunlight across lush forest boards and awaken cute plants.

With every correct reflection, you’ll bring a new flower to life. Watch it bloom in vibrant animations and move on to more intricate levels.

Key Mechanics

Sunlight Source

Every level starts with a fixed light beam you must redirect to the target plant.

Fixed Mirrors

Strategically place angled mirrors (45°, -45°) to bounce the beam through the grid.

Grid & Progression

Play through expanding grids, unlock new tiles, and animate the plant’s growth.
